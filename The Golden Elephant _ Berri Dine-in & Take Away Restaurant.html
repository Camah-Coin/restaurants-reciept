About Us

Tasty, Fresh & Authentic: the way it should be!

We are a local, family owned and operated Dine-in and Take Away Restaurant located in the South Australian food bowl of the Riverland.  Since 2010 we have cultivated an extensive, FRESH, made to order menu full of flavour that invigorates your taste buds. 

Prepared daily from locally acquired produce and meats, we cater to every appetite from Kebabs to Burgers and Pizzas, Seafood, Fish & Chips, Indian cuisine, Gelato, Coffee and Cakes.

Don’t have much time for lunch? Why don’t your try our all day delivery service, or drop in for one of our tasty Lunch Specials.

We also cater for all your family celebrations and work function needs, whether it is in our cosy restaurant or at a venue of your choice.  Don’t have time for decorations?  We do this too.

Seniors specials are also available for the Dine-in Restaurant.

Take a look through our menus, we know you will love them just as much as we do. 

“Arjhan & The Golden Elephant Family”

Menus

At The Golden Elephant our tasty, fresh and authentic menus are designed to give you quality, delicious homemade food.  We are passionate about using local Riverland produce and creating food that is fresh and made from scratch, even our Paneer cheese and wood fire oven pizza dough is made in house by our chefs.

We cater for all dietary requirements, just let us know.

Indian: Our Indian cuisine is cooked to order in our Tandoori oven which gives it a unique flavour to our very popular Butter Chicken and our own creation, the Paradise Chicken.

Oven Pizza:  Pizza isn’t pizza unless its cooked in a wood fire oven.  All your favourites are on the menu and we have even created an Indian inspired ‘Chicken Tikka’ pizza, it’s one of our personal favourites. 

Kebabs & Burgers: Want something quick and on to go? Try our tasty, freshly made kebabs and burgers.   

Seafood, Fish & Chips: Cooked to order, all main seafood meals come with chips and salad.
